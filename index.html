India - States & Union Territories

About India

India is a vast South Asian country with diverse terrain – from Himalayan peaks to Indian Ocean coastline – and history reaching back 5 millennia. It has 28 states and 8 union territories.

Known for its culture, languages, traditions, and democratic structure, India is the largest democracy in the world and one of the fastest-growing economies.
